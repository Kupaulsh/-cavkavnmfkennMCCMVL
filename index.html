Who and Why Finihomes?

Finihomes Residences is a trusted Philippine real estate developer committed to making homeownership accessible to every Filipino family. For over a decade, we have been building quality, affordable condominiums that blend modern design with practical living spaces — all situated in prime locations with easy access to schools, hospitals, transportation, and commercial centers.

Whether you are a first-time buyer, a young professional, or an OFW looking to invest back home, Finihomes offers flexible payment options — including Pag-Ibig financing, bank loans, and in-house terms — so you can move in sooner than you think.
